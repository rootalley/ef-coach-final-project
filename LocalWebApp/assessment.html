Executive Function Skills Assessment

1: I act on impulse.

2: I say things without thinking.

3: I lose things.

4: I have a short temper.

5: I get upset when things don't go as planned.

6: It's hard for me to set priorities when I have a lot of things to do.

7: I run out of energy before finishing a task.

8: My desk or work space is a mess.

9: I have trouble keeping my house or room clean.

10: I have trouble estimating how long it will take to complete a task.

11: I'm slow to get ready for school, work, or appointments.

12: If the first solution to a problem doesn't work, I have trouble thinking of a different one.

13: It's hard for me to put aside fun activities to start things I know I need to do.

14: I have trouble with tasks where I have to come up with my own ideas.

15: I skip checking my work for mistakes, even when the stakes are high.

16: It's hard for me to tell how well I'm doing on a task.

17: I have trouble reaching long-term goals (those that take many weeks or months to finish).

18: I "go with my gut" when making decisions.

19: I get so wrapped up in what I'm doing that I forget about other things I need to do.

20: I have trouble getting back on track if I'm interrupted.

21: Little things frustrate me.

22: I focus on details and miss the big picture.

23: I have trouble making a plan.

24: I get annoyed when tasks are too hard.

25: I live in the moment.
